Alva Skog

Dance Lab 2018

The brief was set by BA Performance: Design & Practice students and staff from Central Saint Martins College. Dance Lab explores some fundamental questions about the dance format: what constitutes ‘dance’, and how the roles of performer, designer, and choreographer intersect & overlap. This year Alva Skog’s work was chosen for its playful illustrations and bold colours which captured the experimental and unconventional spirit of the performances.
