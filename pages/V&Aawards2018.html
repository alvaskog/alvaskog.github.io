The Explorer, the Thinker & the Leader

These three posters was shortlisted in the

V&A Illustration Awards 2018, student category. I was Highly Commended.


In these three posters I explore representations of femininity and female identity positions. Each poster is based on a stereotypically male identity position that is not usually associated with or accessible for women. How can such positions represent women without sexualizing them? I have considered the characters physical positions and I am very conscious of not sexualizing the characters.
