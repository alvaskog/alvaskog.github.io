Margaret Calkin James

This poster is a celebration of Margaret Calkin James (1895 -1985). The poster is a portrait and a timeline of her professional life. This was made for the exhibiton I Don't Know Her Name but I know Her Work that is currently on at Central Saint Martins. The exhibiton is to celebrate under-recognised alumni. December 2017 - February 2018.
